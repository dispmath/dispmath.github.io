For Reviewers

Disputationes Mathematicae welcomes qualified mathematicians who would like to support fair, constructive, and rigorous peer review.

Reviewer Role

Reviewers help the journal assess the originality, clarity, correctness, and mathematical relevance of submitted manuscripts. A good review should be precise, respectful, and useful to both editors and authors.

The journal especially welcomes reviewers with expertise in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, mathematical physics, logic, probability, and related fields with substantial mathematical content.

Review Standards

  • Evaluate manuscripts on scholarly merit and mathematical soundness.
  • Treat submitted work as confidential.
  • Declare possible conflicts of interest before accepting a review request.
  • Provide constructive comments within the agreed review period.

Become a Reviewer

Researchers interested in joining the journal's reviewer pool are invited to contact the Editor-in-Chief directly by email.

The message should include the applicant's name, institutional affiliation, research interests, areas of expertise, and, where available, a link to an academic profile or personal webpage.

By expressing interest in serving as a reviewer, applicants acknowledge their willingness to:

  • Treat all manuscripts and review materials as confidential.
  • Disclose any potential conflicts of interest before accepting a review assignment.
  • Provide fair, objective, and constructive reviews within a reasonable timeframe.

The Editor-in-Chief will evaluate applications and may contact suitable candidates regarding future review opportunities.
